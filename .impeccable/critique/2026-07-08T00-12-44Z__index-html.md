## Priority Issues

- **[P0] Mobile chrome eats the entire first viewport.** At 375â€“390px, header + search + legend + filter pills consume the full screen height before any restaurant is visible.
  - **Why it matters**: Directly undercuts the stated "decide in seconds" goal for the exact context (mobile, on the go) the app is built for.
  - **Fix**: Collapse the legend behind a toggle/tooltip on mobile; move filters into a single "Filters" sheet instead of an always-expanded pill row.
  - **Suggested command**: `/impeccable adapt`

- **[P1] Add Restaurant modal checkboxes render broken.** `#addForm label { flex-direction: column }` (ID selector) beats `.tag-checks label { flex-direction: row }` (class selector) on specificity â€” every tag checkbox stacks above its label instead of sitting beside it.
  - **Why it matters**: The form used to add every new restaurant to the guide visibly breaks on the exact input that captures its dietary/reservation metadata.
  - **Fix**: Scope `#addForm label` to exclude `.tag-checks`, or raise `.tag-checks label`'s specificity.
  - **Suggested command**: `/impeccable polish`

- **[P1] Card secondary text fails WCAG AA contrast.** Detector evidence: 4.4:1 and 2.6:1 against a required 4.5:1 on `.card-type` and adjacent secondary text â€” a direct violation of DESIGN.md's own stated contrast rule.
  - **Why it matters**: The most common real-world readability failure, and the app's own design spec explicitly bans it.
  - **Fix**: Darken `.card-type` (`#666`) and the `#999` secondary text toward ink until they clear 4.5:1 against their tinted backgrounds.
  - **Suggested command**: `/impeccable audit`

- **[P1] Unsaved edits vanish silently.** Rating a restaurant and checking "We've been here," then navigating back without hitting Save, discards the change with no warning.
  - **Why it matters**: The app's entire value is captured personal opinion (ratings, notes) â€” losing it silently defeats the point.
  - **Fix**: Autosave on change (rating/visited already fire events), or disable/confirm navigation while the form is dirty.
  - **Suggested command**: `/impeccable harden`

- **[P2] Star rating touch target contradicts its own spec.** A code comment and DESIGN.md both claim 44Ă—44px per WCAG 2.5.5, but `.star-rating .star` is actually `min-width/height: 28px`.
  - **Why it matters**: A real gap in exactly the "tapping on a phone in a parking lot" scenario the app is designed around.
  - **Fix**: Raise `.star-rating .star` min-width/height to 44px to match the documented intent.
  - **Suggested command**: `/impeccable polish`
